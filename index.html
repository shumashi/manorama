Our Service

Everyday we work hard to make life of our Patients better and happier

CARDIOLOGY

Manorama Hospital has a dedicated team of cardiologists and cardio thoracic surgeons who work in tandem to serve the patients suffering from heart ailments, with comprehensive and multidisciplinary care. With precise and accurate diagnosis, and world class expertise in treatment methods, our cardiologists provide best in class treatments that save lives.

ORTHOPEDICS

The department of Orthopaedics at Manorama is recognized for its surgical excellence and innovative ability to perform both, simple and complex Orthopaedic surgeries along with the use of advanced cutting edge technology such as computer navigation system, high definition cameras and arthroscopic equipment to perform technically challenging procedures.

SPINE

We have best Doctors for minimally invasive techniques in the management of the degenerative and traumatic affections of spine. The vast majority of back problems can be remedied with non-surgical treatments—often referred to as non-surgical or conservative therapies.

GYNECOLOGY

The Division of Gynecology & Gynec Oncology at Manorama holistic services for reproductive health and all gynecological needs of women in a sophisticated and dedicated manner with highest emphasis on patient safety. Our entire team of Gynecology & Gynec Oncology provides a full range routine care along with procedural and technological advancements to empower women with the wellness they deserve to stay healthy in every phase of their lives.ng

UROLOGY

We have Kidney and Urology is a unique resource for those suffering from diseases of the urinary system, including the kidneys, bladder and prostate gland. As a major tertiary and quaternary care centre, our goal is to deliver the highest level of patient care, consistent with our philosophy of ‘Patient-First’. We are proud to have some of the region’s finest experts consulting with us, covering every major urology and nephrology specialty.

NEUROLOGY

Neurology department of Manorama Hospitals is integrated with dedicated and highly qualified Neurologists and Neurosurgeons who ensure the best patient care for a broad range of neurological diseases and disorders.

GASTROENTEROLOGY

Gastroenterology department is to provide excellent patient care with state-of-the-art diagnostic and therapeutic services. Our team of Gastroenterologists uses the most advanced treatments to diagnose, treat and prevent disorders of the digestive system which include pancreas, liver, gallbladder, esophagus, stomach, small intestine and colon.

EMERGENCY

The doctors of Emergency & Critical Care teams are well-trained and specialized in advanced life support, and life-saving techniques. The department treats various patients with critical condition and is responsible for performing the advanced Emergency and Critical care services to save the lives of severely injured.
